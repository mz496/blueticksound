blue tick is an electronic pop music project by producer and classically-trained instrumentalist Matthew Zhu.

The blue tick represented absolutely nothing until the early 2010s when it became a symbol of endorsement and influence online, and then it meant absolutely everything.

Co-opting the idea upfront is just old becoming new again, and new becoming old again.
